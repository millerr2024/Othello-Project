How to Play Othello

Othello is a two player game. Players take turns placing their discs on the board.
The goal of Othello is to have the most discs of your color on the board at the end of the game.
A legal move is any placement that sandwiches at least one of your opponent's discs vertically, horizontally, or diagonally between the disc you're placing and an existing disc of your color.
After you make your move, any opponent discs that become sandwiched between your newly placed disc and an existing disc of yours will be flipped to your color.
You must place a disc if there exists a legal move. If you have no legal moves, the game will notify you and you will pass your turn. The same can happen for your opponent.
The game ends when there are no more legal moves for either player or when the board is full.
The winner is the player with the most discs of their color on the board at the end of the game.
